KNOW YOUR AKAN NAME.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana ,children are give their first name as a 'day name'. This corresponds to the day in the week they were born . The following are the names and their corresponding days of the week:

The female names include:
Monday:Adjoa
Tuesday:Abena
Wednesday:Akua
Thursday:Yaa
Friday:Afua
Saturday:Ama
Sunday:Akosua

The male names include:
Monday:Kojo
Tuesday:Kwabena
Wednesday:Kwaku
Thursday:Yaw
Friday:Kofi
Saturday:Kwame
Sunday:Kwes
